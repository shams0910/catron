Publish rules
In creation page author selects one of two options:
1. publish now
2. save draft
If article has published date then it is considered as completed article
otherwise it will be went to drafts


Draft section:
While editing drafted article author again has 2 options:
1. publish now
2. save draft

Published date will be auto assigned

Creation of category will be available while creating or editing the article

Edit completed article:
In that case "save draft" option will not be available.
In future:
While saving the published article as draft, a new article instance will be created with no published date and that instance will be in draft section. When that instance is published it will override the old existing one.
